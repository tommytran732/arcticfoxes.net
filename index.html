ArcticFoxes

This is a small Matrix homeserver for cool nerds to hang around.

Rules: Do not post illegal materials, spam, or harrass other people.

Privacy: We do not sell your data to third parties. However, we do have the data that every Matrix homeserver operator has access to, including but not limited to your IP address, your contacts, which rooms you are in, etc. We reserve the right to forward criminal activities to relevant authorities and comply with lawful requests where there is legal basis.

Hosting: The server runs on our own hardware with drive encryption and colocated in the UK. Static pages are hosted on Cloudflare, but anything involving your private data such as the Element web client or the API endpoints are not behind their reverse proxy.
